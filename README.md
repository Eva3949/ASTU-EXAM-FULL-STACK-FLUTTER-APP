# 🚀 ASTU-EXAM-FULL-STACK-FLUTTER-APP

## 📌 Overview
ASTU EXAM APP is your ultimate tool for exam preparation. Access past papers, take mock exams, and track your progress with our intuitive and powerful features.

ASTU E-Portal
Direct access to the official Adama Science and Technology University student portal.

GPA Calculator
Easily calculate your semester and cumulative GPA to track your academic standing.

Code Runner
Run and test your Python and C++ code snippets directly within the app.

Announcements
Stay updated with the latest news and announcements from the university.

AI Assistant
Get instant help and guidance for your exam preparations with our smart AI assistant.

Exams
Access mid and final exams for FreshMan 1, FreshMan 2, and SOEEC students.
